Oatmeal

Bananapan
Ingredients: oats, milk, water and a dash of salt. The ratio for oats and liquids is 1:2.
Ingredients: 1 egg, 1 banana, 1 tea spoon baking powder, 1 spoon cinnamon
Instructions: The usual serving size is 1/2 cup oats to 1 cup of water, milk or combination of both.
Instructions: Mash the banana. Mix everything together and put oil in the pan.
Then place in the microwave without a cover on it. Microwave on high for 2 minutes and it should be perfect.
Make small pancakes as the mixture is not very thick and it may be hard to flip them.

Chicken with samardala

"Shopsko" cheese
Ingredients: Chicken breasts,flour, samardala
Ingredients: 200g bulgarian cheese, tomato sause, 1 egg
Instructions: Wash the chicken breasts and put the mix of flour and samardala all over them.
Instructions: Put everything (in the order cheese, sause, egg) in an oven-friendly bowl. Cook at 200 degrees.
Cooking time: 10 minutes

Apple fruit pizzas

Peanut-butter and banana ice cream
Ingredients: 1 apple, sliced crosswise into 8 slices, almond butter, mini chocolate chips, roasted pistachios, honey
Ingredients: 2 bananas, 100 g peanut butter
Instructions: Spread each apple slice with 1 tablespoon almond butter. Top with 1 tablespoon chocolate chips, 1/2 teaspoon pistachios and 1/2 teaspoon honey.
Instructions: Place bananas and peanut butter in a food processor. Pulse and process until mostly smooth, then put in the freezer.
